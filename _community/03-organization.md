## The OpenPGP Organization

The OpenPGP Organization was set up by members of the OpenPGP community attending the [2022 Email Summit](/community/email-summit/2022/minutes/#keys.openpgp.org_Governance).
It was initially called "the keys.openpgp.org Organization" and was intended to provide community governance of the then-new [keys.openpgp.org](https://keys.openpgp.org) keyserver.

In 2026, following a poll of the voting members, it was renamed to [the OpenPGP Organization](http://gitlab.com/keys.openpgp.org/governance) and given oversight of the [OpenPGP.org](https://openpgp.org) website and domain.

The OpenPGP.org website is operated by the [members of the OpenPGP GitHub organization](https://github.com/orgs/OpenPGP/people).
The OpenPGP.org domain registration is held in trust by the [Wau Holland Stiftung](https://wauland.de) on behalf of the community.
