Kleopatra is a certificate manager and GUI for GnuPG [^gpg]. The software stores your OpenPGP certificates and keys. It is available for Windows and Linux. In association with the [KMail]( https://www.kde.org/applications/internet/kmail2/) email client, you can also take advantages of the cryptographical features for your communication via email.
